LKY Hotels are the King of the hotels, literally and metaphorically. We began our journey 25 years back and now we are proud owners of hotels at different locations.

LKY Hotels are synonymous to a modern home on highways. Our hotel is laced with all the modern amenities, from Kid’s play area, shopping hubs and game zones to in-house luxurious dining – we have something for every member of your family.

LKY Hotels are located at more than 5 locations, and if you are on the Jaipur-Delhi highway, chances are, we will find you. After all, how can you miss LKY the Luxury?
